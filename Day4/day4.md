🚀 Day 4/60 — Learning Chain-of-Thought Prompting



Most people use AI to get quick answers.

Today, I learned how to use AI to solve problems more strategically.

Instead of asking a simple question, I guided Claude through a step-by-step thinking process.



The result?

✅ Identified my current skill gaps

✅ Suggested relevant projects

✅ Created actionable milestones

✅ Built a personalized roadmap aligned with my goals

What surprised me most was the difference in quality.

A basic prompt produced general suggestions.

A Chain-of-Thought prompt produced a structured plan with clear next steps.



💡 Lesson of the day:

AI is far more powerful when you guide its reasoning, not just request an answer.The better the prompt structure, the better the outcome.
